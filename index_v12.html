    Labirinto della Masone

    Designed by Franco Maria Ricci, Labirinto della Masone is a complex covering around eight hectares. The avenues of the labyrinth have been created using a particular species of bamboo called Phyllostachys Bissetii, but different kinds of bamboo species can be found in the small squares, where visitors can acquaint themselves better with the varieties of this surprising plant.

  • Country

    Minotaur Maze at Kielder

    Located in the grounds of Kielder Castle. Wheelchair users can access the work via the Duchess trail. The Minotaur Maze is a must for children and adults alike. The adventurous will discover a number of special features that include a set of stairs taking visitors above the walls and enabling them to consider possible alternative routes within the maze.

  • Country

    Tasmazia & The Village of Lower Crockpot

    The complex consists of 8 mazes, including The Great Maze – at the time of planting in 1987 the world’s largest – as well as The Village of Lower Crackpot, a whimsical model village built to 1/5th scale, Embassy Gardens, a self serve Cafeteria, Gift Shop, Lavender Farm, and ever present views of majestic Mount Roland.
